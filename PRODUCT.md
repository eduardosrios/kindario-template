# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Kindario is for donors, volunteers, local partners, and nonprofit teams reviewing a front-page website template for humanitarian giving. Visitors arrive with limited time and need to understand what help is needed, how support moves, and why the organization can be trusted.

## Product Purpose

The template presents a charity, NGO, or humanitarian organization through a polished public-facing landing page. Success means the page feels credible, specific, and emotionally clear while making donation, volunteer, campaign, and field-report paths easy to scan.

## Brand Personality

Direct, field-grounded, and warm. The brand should feel active and accountable rather than sentimental, generic, or decorative.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic charity landing-page skeletons, repeated numbered section scaffolding, beige editorial sameness, soft AI card grids, fake SaaS polish, over-rounded ghost cards, stock-looking decorative layouts, and any design that ignores strong screenshot references.

## Design Principles

- Let real imagery and concrete field copy carry trust.
- Make the first screen feel intentionally art-directed, not assembled from default sections.
- Use one confident visual idea per fold instead of repeating the same card rhythm.
- Keep donation and volunteer paths visible without making the page feel transactional.
- Prefer specific proof, routes, and field notes over broad inspirational claims.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and interactive controls. Keep keyboard focus visible, preserve reduced-motion behavior, use meaningful image alt text, and avoid hiding essential content behind animation.
