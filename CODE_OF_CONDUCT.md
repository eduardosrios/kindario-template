# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in Kindario a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respecting different viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback with care.
- Focusing discussion on what is best for the project and its users.
- Showing empathy toward other community members.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately through the repository maintainer contact listed in the Security Policy or through the repository owner on GitHub.

All reports will be reviewed and addressed as fairly and promptly as possible.
