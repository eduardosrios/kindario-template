Questions

Clear answers before people commit time or money.

How does Kindario decide which needs appear first?

Needs are ranked by field urgency, partner readiness, budget clarity, and whether donor support can move the next step within a defined window.

Can volunteers help without donating?

Yes. Volunteer shifts, transport support, sorting days, and local partner introductions are listed as separate support routes.

What does a donor receive after supporting?

Supporters receive campaign updates, field-note summaries, and delivery confirmation when the local team closes the activity.
