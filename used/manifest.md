## Build Rule

The page must be led by the selected screenshots, not by a generic charity landing-page skeleton. The rejected pattern is also explicit: do not use a three-card dark-green feature row with small icon dots, and do not reuse copy or structure similar to "Transparent giving / Field-led priorities / Community accountability".

## Extracted Direction

The template should feel like an editorial field report crossed with a giving console. It should use asymmetric poster layouts, mixed-width boards, campaign ledgers, image mosaics, and compact utility strips. The page should avoid the old split hero, centered top navigation rhythm, large pill CTA as the main identity, equal three-card feature rows, and any one-note green palette.

### Hero

Dominant references: `screenshot_image_125.png`, `screenshot_image_137.png`, `screenshot_image_159.png`, `screenshot_image_173.png`, `screenshot_image_175.png`.

Use a cream editorial canvas with a tall utility rail, large serif headline, orange signal copy, layered photo blocks, and small operational status modules. The hero should read as a magazine cover, not a standard text-left/image-right landing page.

### Section 1: Field Priority Board

Dominant references: `screenshot_image_223.png`, `screenshot_image_140.png`, `screenshot_image_104.png`, `screenshot_image_184.png`.

Use mixed-width mission cards, one image-backed card, one yellow action card, and dense status labels. Avoid three equal dark cards.

### Section 2: Campaign Ledger

Dominant references: `58.jpg`, `59.jpg`, `screenshot_image_57.png`, `screenshot_image_102.png`, `screenshot_image_178.png`.

Use progress meters, amount chips, campaign targets, raised values, and compact campaign summaries. The section should feel accountable and transactional without becoming a fake payment flow.

### Section 3: Proof Wall

Dominant references: `52.png`, `screenshot_image_121.png`, `screenshot_image_104.png`, `screenshot_image_140.png`.

Use uneven evidence strips, large numbers, compact labels, and small field captions. Do not make icon feature cards.

### Section 4: Story Mosaic

Dominant references: `screenshot_image_218.png`, `screenshot_image_217.png`, `screenshot_image_120.png`, `screenshot_image_122.png`.

Use a mosaic of documentary images with field-note metadata and a quote panel. The section should be visual and human, not a generic two-column story block.

### Section 5: Ways To Help Console

Dominant references: `screenshot_image_57.png`, `screenshot_image_147.png`, `screenshot_image_168.png`, `screenshot_image_126.png`.

Use a usable support console with tabs, amount buttons, form fields, and a single final CTA. Keep it illustrative only; no fake payment/account processing.

### Section 6: Volunteer Dispatch

Dominant references: `screenshot_image_185.png`, `screenshot_image_188.png`, `screenshot_image_141.png`, `screenshot_image_130.png`.

Use tile blocks, date/event cards, mixed colors, and action-forward volunteer language. This section should deliberately break out of the calm green palette.

### Section 7: Questions, Notes, Footer

Dominant references: `screenshot_image_117.png`, `screenshot_image_39.png`, `screenshot_image_101.png`, `51.png`.

Use FAQ rows, short field notes, and a structured dark footer with a yellow contact block.
