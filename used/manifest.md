## Direction Extracted

The Kindario front page should feel like a premium humanitarian organization: restrained off-white and deep forest surfaces, confident editorial typography, documentary human imagery, clear support pathways, compact trust cues, and transparent non-transactional calls to action. The result must avoid generic charity icon grids, exploitative poverty framing, fake payment interfaces, exaggerated claims, and decorative-only visuals.
